Camera Permission Help

If you've denied camera access, please:

  1. Look for a camera icon in your browser's address bar and click it
  2. Select "Allow" for camera access
  3. Refresh the page and try again
  4. Alternatively, check your browser settings to reset permissions for this site

How to use:

  1. Click "Start Scanner" to activate your camera
  2. Allow camera access when prompted
  3. Point your camera at a QR code

  5. Click "Stop Scanner" to turn off the camera
  6. Use "Switch Camera" to toggle between front and rear cameras
